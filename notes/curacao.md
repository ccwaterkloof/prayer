# People profile

Located off the coast of Venezuela and just outside the South Caribbean hurricane belt,
Curacao has been a constituent nation in the Kingdom of the Netherlands since 2010 and is
the largest populated island in the area.

The discovery of oil in the Maracaibo Basin in 1914 transformed Curaçao into a major
refinery location, altering its economic landscape.

Curacao has a free port and is home to the largest ship repair dock in the Americas. It
also has one of the highest standards of living in the Caribbean. With its semi-arid
climate, consistently warm temperatures, and a rainy season that supports sparse
vegetation, the island is well suited to tourism, which accounts for 84% of the economy.

  church.

  renewing the Christian population.
